iPad 11th Gen vs Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 FE

Two top tablets for students and creators. We reviewed display quality, note-taking, editing performance, battery life, and value to help you pick the right one.

Buy on Store

Product Overview

iPad 11th Gen is best for users already in the Apple ecosystem who need fluid app optimization, long software support, and reliable performance for LumaFusion and Procreate.

Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 FE offers strong value with an included S Pen, expandable storage, and DeX multitasking for productivity-focused students.

Who Should Buy

  • Buy iPad 11th Gen if you want top-tier app optimization and better resale value.
  • Buy Galaxy Tab S9 FE if you want the best accessories/value bundle out of the box.
  • Choose either for notes, lectures, and light-to-mid video editing tasks.

Pros

  • iPad 11th Gen: Faster export times in common creator apps.
  • Galaxy Tab S9 FE: Included S Pen lowers initial setup cost.
  • Both: Excellent displays for study and content consumption.

Cons

  • iPad 11th Gen: Accessories like keyboard and pencil are extra purchases.
  • Galaxy Tab S9 FE: Some editing apps are less optimized than iPad versions.
  • Both: Base storage tiers can fill quickly for video-heavy creators.

Comparison Section

In export testing with short 1080p edits, iPad 11th Gen finished faster and had smoother timeline playback in most creator apps. Galaxy Tab S9 FE was close for light edits and delivered better value when factoring in included stylus and storage expansion.

If your priority is professional editing apps and long-term app ecosystem support, go with iPad 11th Gen. If you need the strongest student package on a tighter budget, Galaxy Tab S9 FE is the better buy.
